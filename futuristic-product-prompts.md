# Futuristic Product-System Prompts

## 1. Modular Technology Ecosystem

```text
Create a premium campaign visual for [PRODUCT OR PLATFORM].

Concept:
Present the product as the central intelligence inside a larger modular system.
Surround it with connected components, interface fragments, structural frames,
and material layers that feel purposeful rather than decorative.

Composition:
Place the main product slightly off-center with a clear hierarchy between the
core object, supporting modules, and background system. Use controlled depth,
clean alignment, and generous negative space for typography.

Materials:
Brushed metal, matte polymer, precision glass, translucent acrylic, and subtle
illuminated edges. Every material should respond realistically to light.

Typography:
Use bold, flat, modern sans-serif typography. Keep labels minimal and technical.
Avoid fake 3D text, excessive glow, random UI panels, and unreadable microcopy.

Quality controls:
Accurate perspective, coherent reflections, realistic scale, clean geometry,
purposeful interface elements, no floating decorative objects, and no generic
science-fiction clutter.
Design a futuristic industrial campaign for [BRAND OR PRODUCT].

Creative direction:
Show the product operating inside a highly controlled technical environment,
where every structural element communicates precision, reliability, and advanced
engineering.

Composition:
Use a strong central axis, low camera angle, layered foreground architecture,
and one clear visual pathway leading toward the product.

Lighting:
Directional white light, subtle amber or blue system indicators, controlled
reflections, deep shadows, and restrained atmospheric depth.

Typography:
Use a disciplined grid with one large headline, one technical proof point,
and one compact product description.

Quality controls:
No random holograms, no excessive neon, no cluttered dashboard overlays,
no unrealistic reflections, and no generic cyberpunk styling.
Create a campaign visual where [PRODUCT] becomes a transparent interface object.

Concept:
The product should appear as a physical system made from layered glass,
translucent membranes, and embedded information surfaces.

Composition:
Use one dominant object with internal depth. Interface elements should appear
integrated into the material rather than floating independently.

Lighting:
Soft directional studio lighting, realistic refraction, subtle caustics,
controlled highlights, and accurate material thickness.

Typography:
Minimal technical labels, large confident headline, and clean alignment.

Quality controls:
Readable hierarchy, believable glass behavior, realistic shadows, no excessive
glow, no random particles, and no decorative UI without purpose.
Create a futuristic product architecture poster for [PRODUCT].

Show the product as an engineered system with separated layers, modular parts,
and clearly organized functional zones.

Use an exploded-view composition without making it feel like a conventional
technical manual. Balance precision with premium advertising art direction.

Include:
- One dominant product view
- Three functional annotations
- One large campaign statement
- One technical data point

Use a restrained palette, strong grid, crisp typography, and physically coherent
materials.

Avoid clutter, fake complexity, excessive labels, inconsistent scale,
and random interface decoration.
