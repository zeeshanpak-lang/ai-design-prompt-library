# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors should create a welcoming, respectful, and constructive environment.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Giving specific and useful feedback
- Respecting different skill levels and backgrounds
- Crediting other contributors
- Focusing criticism on the work rather than the person

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory
language, deliberate plagiarism, spam, or publishing private information.

Project maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that do not follow
these standards.
