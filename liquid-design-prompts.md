# Liquid and Transparent Design Prompts

## 1. Transparent Material Identity

```text
Create a campaign visual for [BRAND/PRODUCT] using a transparent material system.

Build one sculptural form from glass, gel, acrylic, or liquid membrane. The form
must refract and distort nearby typography in a physically believable way.

Use controlled studio lighting, accurate caustics, realistic thickness, coherent
reflections, and a restrained background. Avoid random bubbles, excessive chrome,
and decorative liquid splashes without purpose.
```

## 2. Liquid Typography

```text
Create the word “[WORD]” as a semi-transparent liquid object.

The letterforms should retain excellent readability while stretching, merging,
and reacting to gravity. Use realistic surface tension, thickness variation,
internal reflections, and contact shadows.

Place the typography within a minimal [COLOR] environment with one product or
subject. Make the liquid behavior support [CAMPAIGN MESSAGE].
```

## 3. Product Inside a Membrane

```text
Create a futuristic campaign where [PRODUCT] is suspended inside a transparent,
flexible membrane.

The membrane should compress around the product and reveal its silhouette through
refraction and tension. Use physically coherent highlights, soft environmental
shadows, and purposeful negative space for typography.

The result should feel like a premium material experiment, not a generic AI blob.
```
