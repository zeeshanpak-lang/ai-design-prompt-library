# Editorial Advertising Prompts

## 1. Monumental Type Campaign

```text
Create a vertical [RATIO] editorial campaign for [BRAND/PRODUCT].

Concept:
The campaign is built around one monumental word: “[WORD].” The typography acts
as architecture rather than decoration, partially framing and obscuring the subject.

Composition:
Use a disciplined magazine grid with a confident subject positioned off-center.
Allow generous negative space. Add only two supporting information blocks:
[SUPPORTING MESSAGE] and [CALL TO ACTION].

Art direction:
Flat, bold sans-serif typography; crisp alignment; restrained [COLOR 1],
[COLOR 2], and [ACCENT] palette; subtle print texture; no bevels, fake 3D text,
or cluttered dashboard elements.

Photography:
Natural commercial portrait lighting, realistic skin texture, coherent shadows,
and believable depth between the subject and typography.

Final quality:
Global campaign standard, art-directed rather than AI-decorated, readable type,
accurate anatomy, and no generic stock-photo pose.
```

## 2. Editorial Product Dossier

```text
Design a premium editorial product dossier for [PRODUCT].

Show one large hero image and three tightly cropped material studies. Use captions
that explain [FEATURE 1], [FEATURE 2], and [FEATURE 3] as if they belong to a
high-end industrial design magazine.

Use a strict modular grid, understated typography, technical numbering, and
controlled contrast. The page should feel collectible and intelligent—not like
an e-commerce listing.
```

## 3. Founder and System

```text
Create an editorial founder campaign for [COMPANY].

Position the founder naturally inside the working environment rather than against
a generic studio background. Surround the portrait with a minimal visual system
showing one product, one process, and one proof point.

Use confident typography, documentary realism, and a restrained palette. Match
the subject’s lighting and color temperature to the environment so the person
does not look pasted into the scene.
```
