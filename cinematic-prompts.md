# Cinematic Campaign Prompts

## 1. Cinematic Product Arrival

```text
Create a cinematic [RATIO] product campaign for [PRODUCT].

Scene:
The product emerges into a real environment at [TIME OF DAY]. Instead of floating,
it physically affects the scene through [DUST / WATER / LIGHT / FABRIC / DEBRIS].

Camera:
[CAMERA ANGLE], [LENS CHARACTER], controlled depth of field, strong foreground
layering, and realistic scale.

Lighting:
Motivated environmental light with one controlled commercial highlight on the
product. Preserve natural shadows and material response.

Mood:
[EMOTION], premium and restrained. Avoid excessive neon, random particles,
over-sharpening, and artificial glow.

Quality:
Photographic realism, accurate reflections, real material texture, coherent
perspective, and campaign-level composition.
```

## 2. Human-in-Motion Campaign

```text
Create a high-energy campaign featuring [SUBJECT] interacting with [PRODUCT].

Capture a decisive moment rather than a posed jump. Use asymmetric body movement,
fabric tension, directional motion blur, and environmental reaction to communicate
speed.

Make [PRODUCT] the visual priority through perspective and lighting, but keep human
anatomy believable. Hands, fingers, joints, clothing folds, and contact shadows must
remain accurate.
```

## 3. Quiet Cinematic Story

```text
Create a quiet cinematic advertisement for [BRAND].

Show a single human moment that communicates [MESSAGE] without overt selling.
Use natural window light, controlled production design, subtle film grain,
and one brand-colored detail.

The image should feel like a frame from a carefully directed film—emotionally
specific, visually restrained, and free from generic advertising expressions.
```
